Frequently Asked Questions

How does Countryle work? ‚ñº

Countryle presents you with a mystery country each day. You have 7 attempts to guess the country using clues about its continent, population, GDP, language, and climate. After each guess, you'll receive color-coded feedback to help guide your next guess.

What do the colors mean? ‚ñº

Green indicates a perfect match, yellow shows a partial match or close proximity, and red means the information is incorrect. Use these colors to strategically narrow down possibilities.
